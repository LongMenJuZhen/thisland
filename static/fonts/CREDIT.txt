EndfieldByButan.ttf
====================

Author: 罗醭坦 (@lhclbt on GitHub / B 站)
Source: https://github.com/lhclbt/Endfield_Font
License: CC BY-NC 4.0 (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc/4.0/)

This font is a re-creation of the original writing system used in
Hypergryph's games Arknights and Arknights: Endfield, decoded from
in-game and promotional materials. It may be used/re-created freely
for NON-COMMERCIAL purposes only.

This personal blog is non-commercial; the font is used to render
the "Sarkaz" (萨卡兹) fictional language variant of the English
content. All credit remains with the original author.
